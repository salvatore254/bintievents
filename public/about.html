About Binti

From a small team to one of Kenya’s leading event specialists — we craft memorable experiences.

Our Story

Binti Tents & Events began with a passion for beautiful celebrations. Over the years we've grown to serve weddings, corporates, festivals and brand activations across Kenya — investing in premium inventory and experienced crews to deliver professional results every single time.

Our Vision

To be East Africa's most trusted and innovative event partner, known for flawless execution and creating unforgettable experiences.

Our Mission

Deliver beautiful, flawless events with thoughtful service, reliable logistics, and premium equipment that exceed expectations every time.
