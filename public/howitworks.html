How It Works

A simple, stress-free process from package selection to celebration

Five Simple Steps

1

Choose Your Package

Browse our curated private or corporate event packages. Each is designed to deliver stunning results without complexity. Pick the tier that matches your vision and budget.

What's Included: Furniture, styling, setup and breakdown. Prices listed as "from..." based on guest count.
2

Select Your Party Size

Choose your guest count. Our packages scale in batches of 10 guests. If the standard package size doesn't match, we adjust pricing accordingly.

Private Events: Up to 30, 60, or 150+ guests
3

Add Your Extras

Customize with add-ons like lighting, PA sound, dance floor, stage, welcome signs, table centrepieces, and more.

Easy Upsells: Our extras are clearly priced with no surprises.
4

Share Your Event Details

Fill in your event information: date, time, location, contact details, and any special requests. This ensures our team can plan perfectly for your event.

What We Confirm: Availability, delivery logistics, and all setup details.
5

Secure Your Booking

Pay a deposit (M-Pesa or card) to secure your booking. Balance due before your event. Confirmation sent via email.

Payment Options: M-Pesa or Card. Flexible terms available.

Common Questions

How quickly can we get setup?

We typically schedule setup on the morning of your event. Our team arrives 2-3 hours before your event starts to ensure everything is perfect.

Can we customize colors or themes?

Our packages come in elegant neutral tones. Custom colors and themes can be accommodated as extras or through our custom package option, quoted separately.

What if our guest count changes?

No problem! We adjust pricing based on actual guest count in batches of 10. Just let us know before the event date.

Do we include transportation setup?

Yes, standard delivery within Nairobi is included. Delivery outside service areas may incur additional charges.

What if I need something not in the packages?

Our Custom Package option is perfect for unique requests. Fill out a form, we'll discuss your needs, and provide a tailored quote.
