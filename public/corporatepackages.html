Corporate FAQ

How do we get a custom quote?

Request a proposal using the form below. Tell us about your event, guest count, venue, and specific needs. Our team will contact you within 24 hours with a tailored quote.

What's the typical lead time?

Standard lead time is 2-3 weeks. For rush events, we'll do our best to accommodate. Contact us to discuss your timeline.

Do you handle multiple events simultaneously?

Yes, we can manage multiple events on the same day or weekend with our trained setup teams. Availability depends on your dates.

Can we include our company branding?

Absolutely. We provide branded backdrops and signage stands. You supply your branding materials or we can coordinate with your designer.

Are outside venues and locations covered?

Yes. Standard delivery is within Nairobi. Outside service areas may incur delivery charges. We'll confirm logistics when we discuss your proposal.

What if we need additional services?

Add-ons like catering coordination, floral design, or additional staff can be arranged. We'll include these in your custom quote.
